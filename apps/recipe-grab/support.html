Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your recipes, grocery lists, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. Deleting the app removes all data permanently.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions to view, modify, or cancel your subscription. Changes take effect at the end of the current billing period.

Why did a recipe import fail or look wrong?

Recipe extraction uses AI to parse web pages, which works well for most recipe sites but can occasionally misinterpret unusual layouts. Try re-importing the recipe, or manually edit the extracted content within the app.

How do I request a feature or report a bug?

Send us an email at hello@thirdorbitlabs.com with a description of what you'd like to see or what went wrong. Screenshots are always helpful!
